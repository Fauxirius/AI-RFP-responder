# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Style:** Professional and Clean. The interface should prioritize clarity and usability, suitable for an enterprise environment.
- **Theme Reference:** While "Crystal Teal" is the current implementation, future iterations should ensure the design remains accessible and not overly distracting, maintaining a professional aesthetic.
- **Typography:** Legible, sans-serif fonts (e.g., 'Plus Jakarta Sans' as currently used) to ensure readability of long-form text.

## Tone of Voice (Generated Content)
- **Primary Tone:** Formal and Authoritative. Responses should exude confidence and expertise.
- **Secondary Tone:** Professional and Objective. Avoid overly salesy language; focus on facts and capabilities.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Efficiency:** Minimize clicks to perform core actions like parsing and generation.
- **Transparency:** Clearly indicate where information is coming from (citation of knowledge base sources).
- **Feedback:** Provide immediate visual feedback for system status (loading, processing, success, error).
